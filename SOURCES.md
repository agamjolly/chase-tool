# Sources

- [Chase Sapphire Reserve official card page](https://creditcards.chase.com/rewards-credit-cards/sapphire/reserve)
  - Shows the `$500` The Edit credit and the separate `$250` credit through `12/31/26`.
- [The Edit by Chase Travel: What to know](https://www.chase.com/personal/credit-cards/education/basics/the-edit-by-chase-travel-what-to-know)
  - Explains that The Edit is accessed inside Chase Travel and highlights the standard on-property benefits.
- [How to Use the $500 The Edit by Chase Travel Credit](https://www.chase.com/personal/credit-cards/education/chase-cards/sapphire-reserve-for-business-hotel-statement-credit)
  - Useful for the 2-night minimum and split-credit mechanics.
- Public Chase hotel guides and spotlights used for cross-checking property names:
  - [Hotel guides index](https://www.chase.com/travel/guide/hotel-guides/)
  - [Hotel spotlights index](https://www.chase.com/travel/guide/hotel-spotlights/)

## Mirrored property catalog

- [hotels-with-the-edit.replit.app](https://hotels-with-the-edit.replit.app/)

- Chase names the seven official `$250` partner groups on the Sapphire Reserve page, but does not publish a full public property-level roster for that benefit.
- The mirror site appears to use `hotel-content-graphics.cnxloyalty.com` image URLs, which suggests a travel-content feed rather than hand-built entries.
